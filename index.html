Hello world!!! My name is Kateryna!

I am an adventurous and passionate traveller. Last 8 years I spent travelling around the world and working in different countries on three continents. During this time I visited around 20 countries. Being intrested in exploring new traditions and customs I collect rare recipes and try to cook them at home. In this way I combine my two main hobbies - travelling and cooking.

I am collecting interesting recipes which are the part of rich heritage from the countries like hospital and mysterios Myanmar, beautiful Cyprus, friendly Georgia, elegant France and many others.

I am from Ukraine, the country which can be truly named as a place of hospitality, rich traditions and friendly people. Amazingly tasty cuisine is a part of national heritage of Ukrainians. I am fond of my national cuisine and like to cook traditional Ukrainian meals.

I truly like to spend my time reading books. I have a plenty of my favourite books among the world and Russian classics. My favourite books are: "The Master and Margarita" by M.Bulgakov, "Idiot" by F.Dostoevsky, "Gone with the wind" by M.Mitchell and detective novels by A.Christie.
